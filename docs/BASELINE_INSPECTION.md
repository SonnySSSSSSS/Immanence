---

## Validation Criteria

Each baseline must pass ALL checks:

- ✅ **Jewel Authority**: Feels manufactured, not illustrated
- ✅ **Single Object**: One continuous jewel, no fragmentation
- ✅ **Smooth Silhouette**: No protrusions, spokes, or radial appendages
- ✅ **Ring Count**: Matches stage specification
- ✅ **Color Palette**: Matches stage specification
- ✅ **No Symbols**: No mandalas, sigils, or sacred geometry
- ✅ **Topology > Beauty**: Topologically correct wins over aesthetically pretty
