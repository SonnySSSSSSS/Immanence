# State Training Doctrine

Immanence is a training system for inner agency.
It is not passive wellness content and not only a meditation timer.
Its purpose is to train the ability to detect, regulate, and choose internal state in real time.

## Core Thesis

- Most people live in conditioned trigger-response loops.
- Training creates a workable gap between trigger and action.
- That gap enables values-aligned choice under pressure.
- The target is equanimity and agency, not temporary calm.

## Two-Language Model

Use spiritual and scientific language together to preserve depth and clarity.

- Energetic body -> nervous-system state and interoceptive signaling
- Karma loops -> conditioned reactivity patterns
- Detachment -> decentering and perspective shift
- Dharma action -> values-aligned behavior under load

## Training Sequence

1. Regulation: breath and arousal control.
2. Attention: sustained focus and return speed.
3. Awareness: observe sensation, thought, and impulse as events.
4. Reflection: choose response aligned with intention.
5. Integration: apply in work, conflict, temptation, and fatigue.

## What Changes With Practice

- Increased trigger-to-reaction latency
- Faster recovery after activation
- Better attentional stability
- Less compulsive behavior
- More consistent values-aligned action

## Positioning Statement

Immanence is a spiritual-neurocognitive training system that helps users move from reactive loops to deliberate action.

## Promise And Scope

Immanence does not promise perfection or immunity from pain.
It offers a disciplined path to greater agency through repeated state training.
